# Changelog v1.0.2 — We're on Discord, plus Automations & Telegram

By TryPost Product Team • [Release v1.0.2](https://github.com/trypostit/trypost/releases/tag/v1.0.2)

Hey — quick rundown of what shipped this week. But first, the thing I'm most excited about:

## We have a Discord now

We just opened the TryPost community on Discord. It's where we'll share what we're building, take your feature requests, and help each other get more out of the tool. I'll be in there myself, so come say hi and tell me what you want us to build next.

**[Join us on Discord →](https://trypost.it/discord)**

Or just head to [trypost.it/discord](https://trypost.it/discord)

## Automations

You can now hand the posting off to TryPost. Point an automation at a source like an RSS feed, and it'll draft a post in your brand voice and schedule it to your channels on whatever cadence you set. There's a visual editor where you wire up the trigger, conditions, and steps, and tabs to watch each run and see what it actually did.

## Telegram

Telegram is a channel now. Add our bot to your channel or group, drop in the one-time connect code, and you're publishing text and media albums. You'll also see per-post reactions and your subscriber count next to the rest of your analytics.

## Sharper posts on every network

Facebook posts let you pick an aspect ratio now, the same way Instagram does, so your images aren't cropped in ways you didn't choose. And on Bluesky, @mentions and links finally resolve properly — tagging someone no longer throws an "invalid post" error.

## New features

- Build automations that draft and schedule posts for you
- Connect Telegram channels and groups, with reactions and subscriber stats
- Pick the aspect ratio for Facebook image posts
- Mention people and add links on Bluesky

## Fixes

- The calendar no longer shows "no content" on posts that clearly have content
- Renaming a workspace doesn't make you re-fill brand details anymore
- If a payment fails, you keep your access and can just update your card instead of starting a new subscription
- The editor's "Schedule" tab is now called "Channels", which is what it always was
- A handful of TikTok and Instagram posting fixes

Cheers,
Paulo from TryPost.it
